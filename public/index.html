QUALITY ADIRONDACK HOPS

Owl Valley Farm is a family owned hop yard and vegatable farm located in the Adirondacks of Upstate New York. Awarded Warren County Farm of the Year in 2016 we are a one of a kind operation ranging from our two acre hop yard to our 30x72 ft greenhouse

Here on our two acre hop yard we grow both Chinook and Cascade hops. We keep quality top of mind through the whole process of cultivation and harvest. Once harvested we sell our hops to local breweries to be brewed into a variety of tasty pints.

We also have alot more going on at the farm...

GREEN HOUSE

We manufactured our very own green house here at Owl Valley that is 30x72 feet and lets us grow your favorite crops year round!

OUR CROPS

We over a variety of your favorite crops including tomatoes, cabbage, cucumbers, onions, carrots and different peppers!

Livestock

Currently Owl Valley has 60 chickens and 13 Ginnie hens, as well as our LaMancha goat, Twilight! Come by an grab a carton of fresh eggs!

Furry Friends

Make sure to be on the look out for our patrol guards found here at Owl Valley Farm. This includes Maeby, our loveable pup, and Cassidy the Tortoiseshell cat!
