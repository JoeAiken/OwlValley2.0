QUALITY HOP SOURCE IN THE HEART
OF THE ADIRONDACKS

Owl Valley Farm is a family owned hop yard and vegatable farm located in the Adirondacks of Upstate New York. Awarded Warren County Farm of the Year in 2016 we are a one of a kind operation ranging from our two acre hop yard to our 30x72 ft greenhouse

Here on our two acre hop yard we grow both Chinook and Cascade hops. We use our harvester which we manufactured right here on site to collect up our crop. Once harvested we sell our hops to local breweries to be brewed into a variety of tasty pints.
